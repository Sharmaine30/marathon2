About the Project

The aim of the proposed online ticket booking system is to provide customers with a streamlined and safe booking experience by eliminating lines, improving accessibility, and minimizing errors. It also optimizes theater administration, enhancing operational effectiveness and aligning with modern industry standards.

Features

User Login and Registration

It allows the users to register, create an account and to login to access the system's features.

Movie Listings

Enables users to view, available movies with details like genre, cast, director, rating, duration, and synopsis.

Seat Selection

Provides a seat map for the chosen showtime, displaying available, and occupied, allowing users to select multiple seats and shows seat pricing options.
